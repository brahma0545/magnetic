Our Objective

We train you to complete the project on your own, guiding you in each and every stage. We assume that you have never done any project till now and also that you dont have any prior coding experience, and so we teach everything from basics. The projects that are listed are on cutting-edge technologies which has real time applications that makes your project outstanding. These projects will make your resume more appealing and help you in campus placements as well. You will be provided with the best guidance from mentors with industrial experience, who have completed their post graduation from prestigious universities (IISc,IIT,NIT,IIIT). We make sure that you grasp every detail of the project right from the abstract to its design followed by implementation and finally documentation. We break the project into several smaller assignments and provide you with the solutions along with the explanation. By the end of the project you will be confident enough on the knowledge and the skills that you have obtained, which will make your way to the software industry a cake walk.


Note: Since this kind of step by step guidance requires a lot of effort from our team, we are taking only limited number of students on first come first serve basis. So please email us before you pay the fee to know the availability.
